ABOUT US

CHEMORA is a specialty chemical and engineering company delivering reliable, efficient, and sustainable water and process solutions.

Founded as BHAVITHA ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS in 2013, the company evolved into CHEMORA in 2026—a rebranded identity reflecting expanded capabilities, a sharper market focus, and a long-term growth vision.

Inspired by global leaders such as Nalco Water, kurita,SNF,Buckman, Solenis, Kemira, BASF, and CHT—CHEMORA combines international best practices with local engineering strength to deliver solutions that work on the ground.

What sets us apart:
  • Application-driven chemistry, not commodity products
  • Engineered systems designed for real operating conditions
  • Strong OEM and project execution background
  • Faster response, flexible customization, and accountable delivery
  • Make-in-India manufacturing aligned with global standards
