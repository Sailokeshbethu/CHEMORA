WATER AUDITS AND JAR TESTING

CHEMORA provides comprehensive water system audits and laboratory-based jar testing to optimize chemical performance, reduce operating costs, and improve treatment efficiency.
Our water audits evaluate:
  • Raw water quality and variability
  • Chemical dosing accuracy and consumption trends
  • System performance (RO, ETP, WTP, Boilers, Cooling Towers)
  • Compliance with discharge and regulatory norms
  • Sludge generation and chemical wastage

Through controlled jar testing, we scientifically determine the most effective coagulant, flocculant, pH adjustment, and specialty chemical combinations for your system.
This ensures:
  • Reduced chemical overuse
  • Improved clarification and settling
  • Better filtration efficiency
  • Lower sludge handling costs
  • Consistent treated water quality

Our approach is data-driven, not trial-and-error — delivering measurable improvements in performance and cost savings.
