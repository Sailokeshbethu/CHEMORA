About Us

CHEMORA is a speciality chemical and engineering company delivering reliable, efficient, and sustainable water and process solutions.
Founded as BHAVITHA ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS in 2013 , the company evolved into CHEMORA in 2026—a rebranded identity reflecting expanded capabilities, a sharper market focus, and a long-term growth vision.
CHEMORA serves the chemical, water & wastewater, pulp & paper industries, as well as global specialty chemical manufacturers as an OEM partner. Over the years, we have strengthened our engineering, project management, and manufacturing capabilities to deliver cost-effective solutions—on time and within budget.
